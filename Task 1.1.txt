Task 1.1: Functional Requirements

- Add a new contact with fields for first name, last name, company, job title, zero or more phone numbers, and zero or more email addresses.

- Update an existing contact's information including first name, last name, company, job title, phone numbers, and email addresses.

- Delete an existing contact from the system.

- Search for contacts based on first name, last name, company, job title, phone numbers, or email addresses.

- Merge duplicate contacts by comparing first name, last name, company, job title, phone numbers, and email addresses.
